What is ONEGAIT?

ONEGAIT is a developing online recruitment platform that acts as a bridge for companies and job applicants to make their recruitment process quickly and efficiently.By having a standardized test i.e., conducting tests on various domains such as Quantitative Aptitude, Logical Reasoning, Programming, Technical MCQs, and other core-based sections. This test will be conducted across various colleges and even by registering online candidates can take the test individually. Scores will be generated and reports will be updated in the candidate’s profile. Based on the companies percentage criteria candidates can apply for further rounds. The eligible candidates appear for further rounds.

How it works?

For Corporate

ONEGAIT team conducts online tests across various colleges which can test their knowledge from quantitative aptitude to their core field. Corporates can use the score obtained by the candidates for shortlisting them. The eligible candidates can appear for further rounds. The corporate can also use our platform for conducting a customized test for them i.e., you can conduct an online test with your own question pattern across colleges and continue your recruitment process. Use ONEGAIT to find the candidate you are looking for, by spending low cost and time.

For Institute

Institutes can allow their students to take tests online on their premises which will be an opportunity for their students to get placed in various MNC’s. Institutes can also use this platform to conduct a test on your own test pattern which can help institutes to test their students’ knowledge. We help institutes to measure, improve and access the student knowledge by conducting standardized test i.e., conducting tests on various domains such as Quantitative Aptitude, Logical Reasoning, Programming, Technical MCQs, and other core-based sections. 'Continuous Evaluation' program helps institutions to build the skill of your students to compete in the industry and set a benchmark.

For Candidates

Candidates can use this platform efficiently so that they can get placed in various top MNC’s. This test can be taken in their respective college. Once a test is taken a profile is created and scores are updated. Based on this score students can apply for the job post in their profile. This score is valid for one year, students can use this score to apply for various jobs. Candidates can also retake the test if they have a poor score. The new score will be updated and based on that score candidates can apply for the job post.
